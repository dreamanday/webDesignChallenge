Avg Deaths Trends

This visual shows the average deaths per 100,000 pop for most countries in the world for 1990 and then from 2010 to 2016. Here we can see a significant decrease in the variable when we compare most current years with 1990 levels. This however could be due to control of pollution or other factor as increase on health infrastructure, availability and access to medicines etc.
