Summary

The main purpose of this site is to analyze trends and correlations regarding pollution levels and its health related concerns for all the world. Making emphasys on the impact of global accords in pollution levels. This site will study the effects of the ratification of the Kyoto Accords on global pollution levels and pollution related health concenrs. It is our main interest to analyse how effective this multilateral accords are, when in contrast to each nations specific self interest of economic growth and profitability.
