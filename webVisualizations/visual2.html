Avg Pollution Trends

Here we see a map of exposure to contaminant particles for 1990 and from 2010 to 2016. We see a consistent decrease when we take the average for world data, this means that as a whole we are making progress towards a cleaner planet, however we cannot see here individual performance, it is important to take in consideration outliers as well. Also we see an interesting efect in 2015, the world gave up three years worth of progress in a single one, this was due to the infamous diesel gate case. It is amazing to see how corruption, fraud and thirst for growth and profit can get in the way of commitment to the enviroment.
